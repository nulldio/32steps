Override Android's default volume steps. Set your own custom step count from 1 to 1000. No root required.

32steps splits each system volume step into smaller sub-steps using audio effects, giving you real, audible volume changes on every button press.

Works system-wide. YouTube, Spotify, games, everything.

Features:
- Custom volume step count (1-1000)
- Works with hardware volume buttons
- System-wide audio control
- Volume overlay with progress bar
- Survives reboots
- No root required
- No internet permission
- No ads, no tracking
